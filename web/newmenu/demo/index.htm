Demo « jsDOMenuBar

Click on any of the following links to view the demo:

  • Demo 1 (strict, quirks): Two menu bars, one absolutely positioned, one fixed positioned.
  • Demo 2 (strict, quirks): Change the theme of the menu bar and menus without reloading the page.
  • Demo 3 (strict, quirks): Draggable menu bar.
  • Demo 4 (strict, quirks): Static menu bar, whose position on the page "flows" with the document structure.
  • Demo 5 (strict, quirks): Show an icon before the display text of the menu bar item.
  • Demo 6 (strict, quirks): Activate the menu bar by moving the cursor over the menu bar item.

You can also view the demo in frames.
